How does our organisation work?

Here is an overview of how we work with our clients:

Fill up the form in Contact section with the details asked and register yourself. You will get a confirmation email sent to you about your request on the email address you gave on the form.

After registering you can tell us your request and our agents will call you shortly. You can ask them any question regarding the project you are requesting for and clear any doubts you have.

After clearing any doubts with the agent, we can start talking about the estimate amount of the project and we can recommend you some things too that might help you on your project.

When the negotiations are done and the estimate is ready we will send you the documents of the contract on your email. The work on the project will commence within 1-2 weeks after that.
